How to Make a great Pizza

So how do we begin making it?

First you have to make sure your dough is nice and thin. Try using half as much sauce; You should be able to see through it at some points. Then all you have to do is put some cheese and any topings of your choice then cook as hot as you can. And that my friends is how you make a good pizza.

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Dough
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  1. Combine water and yeast in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam
  2. Combine flour and salt together in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook attachment. Pour in yeast mixture. Knead dough until smooth, about 7 minutes.
  3. Grease a large bowl lightly with olive oil. Form dough into a tight ball and lightly grease the top. Place in the bowl; cover loosely with a plastic wrap. Let rise until doubled in volume, about 30 minutes.
  4. Mix crushed tomatoes and pizza sauce together in a bowl to make sauce.
  5. Grease a heavy-gauge rimmed 12x17 inch baking sheet generouly with olive oil. press dough into the bottom. Prick dough all over with a fork. Arrange mozzerella cheese slices over dough ; cover with 1 cup sauce. Sprinkle Pecorino Romano Cheese on top. Drizzle remaining olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Let the pizza rise in a warm area until puffy, about 1 hour
  7. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  8. Bake Pizza on the center rack of the preheated oven until edges are very dark brown but the top is not burnt, 15 to 20 minutes. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes before slicing into squares.
